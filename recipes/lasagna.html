Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe is simple, cheesy, and perfect for family dinners.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Ground beef

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles.
  2. Cook the beef with sauce.
  3. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheese.
  4. Bake for 45 minutes.

Lasagna

Description

A classic Italian lasagna recipe made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Ground beef
  • Onion and garlic

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles.
  2. Prepare the meat sauce.
  3. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheese.
  4. Bake at 180°C for 40 minutes.

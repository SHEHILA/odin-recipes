Pizza Recipe

Description

This homemade pizza has a crispy crust, flavorful sauce, and cheesy goodness!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp dry yeast
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup tomato pizza sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings of choice (vegetables, chicken, etc.)

Steps

  1. Mix yeast, sugar, and water — let it rest.
  2. Make dough, let it rise for 1 hour.
  3. Roll out and spread sauce.
  4. Add toppings and cheese.
  5. Bake at 200°C for 20 minutes.

Lasagna

Description

This Pasta recipe is simple, cheesy, and perfect for family dinners.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • chicken
  • ginger garlic paste

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta.
  2. Cook the chicken with masala.
  3. mix pasta, chicken, sauce, and cheese.
  4. Bake for 45 minutes.

Pasta Recipe

Description

This pasta recipe is simple, creamy, and full of flavor. It’s perfect for a quick lunch or dinner and can be customized with your favorite vegetables or chicken.

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta (any type)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup cooked chicken pieces
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated cheese for topping

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water until soft, then drain.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté ginger-garlic paste for 1 minute.
  3. Add cooked chicken and tomato sauce, stir well.
  4. Mix in the boiled pasta and toss until evenly coated.
  5. Sprinkle grated cheese on top and serve hot!

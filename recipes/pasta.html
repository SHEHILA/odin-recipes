Pasta Recipe

Description

This pasta recipe is creamy, flavorful, and perfect for quick dinners!

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Garlic, chopped
  • Vegetables or chicken
  • Tomato or white sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta with salt.
  2. Sauté garlic and veggies.
  3. Add sauce and mix pasta.
  4. Sprinkle cheese and serve warm.
